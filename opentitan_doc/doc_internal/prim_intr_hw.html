Copyright lowRISC contributors. Licensed under the Apache License, Version 2.0, see LICENSE for details. SPDX-License-Identifier: Apache-2.0 Primitive interrupt handler. This assumes the existence of three controller registers: INTR_ENABLE, INTR_STATE, INTR_TEST. This module can be instantiated once per interrupt field, or "bussified" with all fields of the interrupt vector.
